Woodcoin Development Bounties

FAQ Ω Specifications Ω Whitepaper Ω Paper Notes

There is no "official" woodcoin. The coin relies on YOU to do the work of building any infrastructure. It pays you to do so in LOG and with logarithmic appreciation of value.

The KAWG group of LOG stackers also generously offers 100k LOG for the following work, to be rewarded no later than spring soltice 2017.

1) Websites -

Up to 3 awards of 10k LOG will be given out to developers who create sites offering woodcoin wallets, information, and related material.

2) Wallets -

Up to 3 awards of 10k LOG will be given out to developers who curate woodcoin wallets. This could be as simple as maintaining a fork of the funkshelper wallet, complete with binary releases. PPAs and ports are needed. Other projects worthy of the award are a woodcoin-electrum, woodcoin-android, and javascript or html5 wallets.

3) Choppers -

Up to 2 awards of 10k LOG will be given out to developers and publishers of improved woodcutting tools. This includes better pools, skein hashing tools, and possibly more usuable standalone woodcutting clients.

4) Merchants -

Up to 2 awards of 10k LOG will be given out to the best merchant promotion accepting woodcoin.

Other promotions are considered. Write to funkenstein@woodcoin.org if you have a suggestion.
